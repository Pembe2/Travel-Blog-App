Center Parcs

Resort villages with cottages, aqua domes, and family activities close to Germany.

What to expect

Center Parcs resorts are built for low-stress family time with on-site dining, pools, and activities. Most villages are car-free once you park, which keeps kids roaming safely and makes it easy to split up for naps or pool time.

Typical stay setup

  • Self-catering cottages with kitchens and patio space.
  • Market Dome hub for restaurants, cafes, and rainy-day play.
  • Aqua Mundo water park access included for most stays.

Planning tips

  • Book swim sessions early during school holidays.
  • Pack comfy shoes for car-free walks and bike loops.
  • Reserve a grocery delivery or shop on-site for easy meals.
