Val Gardena (Ortisei + Selva), Italy

Dolomite valley base with cable cars, meadow walks, and easy village hopping.

Back to Trips by Car

4 to 5 days

Season

Summer

Family fit

Scenic lifts, short hikes, and meadow picnics

Why families love it

Val Gardena is a Dolomites base where families can combine dramatic ridgelines with gentle meadow walks. Ortisei is compact and easy for strollers, while Selva puts you close to the big hiking starts and cable cars.

  • Seceda ridgeline views
  • Alpe di Siusi meadows
  • Ortisei village strolls
  • Selva di Val Gardena hikes
  • Gardena Pass viewpoints

Suggested 4 to 5 days plan

Day 1

Arrive in Ortisei, explore the pedestrian center, and ride the Seceda cable car for a short ridge walk.

Day 2

Head up to Alpe di Siusi for wide meadow walks and a relaxed hut lunch.

Day 3

Base in Selva and ride the Col Raiser or Dantercepies lifts for scenic loops.

Day 4

Drive the Gardena Pass for viewpoints, then stop in St. Christina for an easy afternoon stroll.

Getting there

Val Gardena is best reached by car. Expect a full-day drive from western Germany with mountain roads for the last stretch; arrive before evening to check in and park.

Family travel tips

  • Use cable cars to skip steep climbs with kids.
  • Base in Ortisei for easier parking and village walks.
  • Start early for Seceda to avoid afternoon clouds.
