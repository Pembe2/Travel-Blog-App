Copenhagen, Denmark

Copenhagen is a compact harbor city where families can combine castles, playgrounds, and waterfront walks without long transfers. Kids love Tivoli Gardens rides, the Experimentarium hands-on science museum, and the harbor promenade around Nyhavn and the Little Mermaid. Parks like King's Garden and the Copenhagen Harbor Baths make it easy to mix sightseeing with downtime.

Suggested 4 to 5 days plan

Day 1

Start with Nyhavn and the harbor promenade, then visit Amalienborg Palace and the Little Mermaid.

Day 2

Spend the day in Tivoli Gardens and end with dinner at Tivoli Food Hall.

Day 3

Tour Rosenborg Castle, relax in King's Garden, and pop into the National Gallery (SMK).

Day 4

Head to the Experimentarium, then take an easy harbor stroll and street-food stop at Reffen.

Day 5

Optional day trip to the Louisiana Museum of Modern Art or a beach afternoon north of the city.

Getting there

Fly into Copenhagen Airport (CPH). The M2 metro line runs directly to the city center in about 15 minutes, so you can skip a car and stay close to a metro or S-train stop.

Family travel tips

  • Bike lanes are everywhere, but walking works fine too.
  • Pack a light jacket for coastal breezes.
  • Tivoli is great for an evening visit.
