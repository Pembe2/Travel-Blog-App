Piran, Slovenia

Piran is a compact coastal town with a car-free core, sea views, and short walks that work well for families. Pair the old town with a beach afternoon in Portoroz to keep the pace relaxed.

Optional day trip to nearby coastal towns or salt pans.

Getting there

Fly into Ljubljana and drive about 1 hour 30 minutes to the coast. Park in a garage outside the old town and walk in to avoid narrow streets.

Family travel tips

  • Park outside the old town and walk in.
  • Plan a short wall walk before midday heat.
  • Use nearby Portoroz for sandy beach time.
